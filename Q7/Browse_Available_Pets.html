Description: Beautiful Mercury is ready to go! We responded to a plea on Facebook, a kind neighbor had noticed Mercury outside with an injured front leg. She deserves to live in a quiet home being a sweet companion to someone. She tolerates other cats but would do best as the only spoiled cat in the household.
